School Projects

Recent Projects I did in class

These are the class projects that I have done in the past few semesters.

Shaft Analysis

We had to design a shaft to widthstand certain kinds of loading. The problem statement is available here. Myself and Stephan(classmate), wrote a MATLAB script to test for static and fatigue failure down the length of the rod. We then verified our results using ANSYS. Final report.

Landing Gear Design

We were tasked with designing the landing gear for a DHC-2 Beaver (problem statement). Ian and I, wrote a MATLAB script to design the optimum landing gear based on a given base shape, and we itterated through a few designs. We verified our final designs in ANSYS. Because of the problem setup we get some rather "interesting" designs. The final report is linked here.

ENPH 257 Thermal Lab

In a team of four we found a whole bunch of thermal properties of an Alumnium Rod (problem statement). We used different thermal sensors and designed our experiments. We then took all of our data and compared it to our models that numerically solved the heat equation for our given setups. My final report can be read here.

Analog to Digital Converter

Myself and Charlie McGrawth build an analog to digital converter based on a series of digital logic gates, basic circuit components, and a decade counter.
