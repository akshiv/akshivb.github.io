Autonomous CropSense Moving Robot

This was a project done for ENPH 459 at UBC. Our project was to build a robot that could translate the CropSense (EIS's core product) around Van Belle tree nursery. I worked in a team of three for this, I was responsible primarily for the mechanical systems, and I helped out with some of the software.
