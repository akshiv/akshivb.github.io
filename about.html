I am a software engineer, mechincally minded, and an environmental activist. Originally from New Delhi, I grew up in Vancouver where I attend the University of British Columbia, graduating with a Bachelors of Applied Science in Engineering Physics and a Minor in Honors Mathematics. Recently, I worked for Advanced Development at Auris Health in the Tools and Infrastructure Team developing robotic Apis Before that, I was in the Instruments team as a mechanical engineer, focusing on performance testing.
